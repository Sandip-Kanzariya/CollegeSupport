Scholerships

1. MYSY

    Eligibility Criteria
  • All those students who wish to purpose higher education like diploma courses, engineering, pharmacy courses, medical course etc apply for MYSY Scholership every year.
  • The candidates should be a domicile of the gujrat state.
  • The applicants should have scored at least 80% in their class 10 exams.
  • Those pursuing Diploma to degree(D2D) courses should have scored at least 65% in diploma.
  • The applicants who pursuing MBBS/BDS courses should have scored a minimum of 80% in the class 12 board exam.
  • Students who are pursuing other undergraduate degrees such as BA. BSc, BEd, etc., should have secured at least 80% marks in Class 12.
  • They should be pursuing a first year of the diploma/ degree program.
  • The annual family income of the applicant should not exceed INR 6,00,000 from all sources.
    Rewards and Benefits

    1. MBBS/BDS

  • Students pursuing MBBS/ BDS from the Gujarat Medical Education Research Society (GMERS) will get a scholarship amount of INR 2,00,000 per annum or 50% of the tuition fees, whichever is less.
  • Book and equipment assistance of INR 10,000.
  • Hostel grant of INR 1,200 per month.

    • 2. Engineering, Architecture, Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, Ayurveda, Homeopathy, and Nursing

  • Students pursuing Engineering, Architecture, Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, Ayurveda, Homeopathy, and Nursing will receive a scholarship amount of INR 50,000 per annum.
  • Book and equipment assistance of INR 5,000.
  • Hostel grant of INR 1,200 per month.

    • 3. Diploma courses

  • Students pursuing Diploma courses will get a scholarship amount of INR 25,000 per annum.
  • Book and Equipment assistance of INR 3,000.
  • Hostel grant of INR 1,200 per month.

    • 4. Other Graduation courses such as BEd, BSc, BA

  • Students pursuing BEd, BSc, BA, etc., will receive a scholarship amount of INR 10,000 per annum.
  • Hostel grant of INR 1,200 per month.





2. CMSS

    Eligibility Criteria
  • The candidate should be a citizen of Gujarat state.
  • The candidates who belong to EBC, SC, ST are eligible for the Gujarat Chief Minister Scholarship.
  • A student should have admission in UG or PG courses in Gujarat.
  • Candidates should be passed 10th & 12th with a minimum 60% marks.
  • Students who are doing research studies need a minimum 55% average marks in their academics.
  • The annual family income of any student should be not more than Rs. 4.5 Lakh per annum.
    Rewards and Benefits
  • The government of Gujarat launched CMSS scholarship on 4th February 2022.
  • Through this scheme financial assistance will be provided to those students who want to pursue higher education.
  • All the students who have secured at least 60% marks in 10th or 12th board examination will be provided benefit of this scholarship scheme.
  • Through this scheme 50% of the fixed annual tuition fees will be provided to the beneficiaries.
  • This scheme will be applicable for those students whose annual family income is upto Rs 4.50 lakh.
  • With the implementation of this scheme students will be able to pursue higher education without worrying about the finances.
  • The beneficiaries of this scheme will also be eligible for existing Mukhyamantri Yuva swavalamban Yojana.






3. Jindal Scholarship

    Eligibility Criteria

    For class 11 and 12

  • The applicants must be studying in class 11 and 12.
  • The male applicants must have scored at least 65% marks in the previous exam.
  • Female candidates must secure a minimum of 60% of marks.
  • Candidates of Karnataka should secure 75% marks for boys and 70% marks for girls.
  • Candidates of West Bengal should secure 70% of marks for boys and 65% marks for girls.
  • In case of those in employment, the annual family income must not be more than INR 4 Lakh.
  • For other students, the annual family income must not be more than INR 2.5 Lakh.
  • The applicants must not have crossed the age of 30 years.

    • Engineering and Medical courses

  • Candidates must be studying in Medical or Engineering, or architecture courses.
  • Male candidates must secure a minimum of 70% of marks.
  • Female candidates must secure a minimum of 65% of marks.
  • Candidates of Karnataka should secure 75% marks for boys and 70% marks for girls.
  • Candidates who are employed must have an annual income of less than Rs. 4 lacs p.a.
  • Other candidates who are not employed should have an annual family income of less than Rs. 2.5 lacs.
  • The candidate ought to be less than 30 years.
    Rewards and Benefits

    For class 11 and 12

  • Rs.500 per month

    • Engineering courses

  • Rs.1700 per month (for girls)
  • Rs.1500 per month (for boys)

    • Medical courses

  • Rs.2000 per month (for girls)
  • Rs.1800 per month (for boys)

    • For students living in a hostel

  • Rs. 800 per month (Additional amount for hostellers studying ITI, Diploma, Postgraduate, Graduate) courses.
  • Rs. 1200 per month (Additional amount for hostellers studying Engineering, Medical, courses including Post Graduate courses.






4. LIC Golden Jubilee Scholarship

    Eligibility Criteria
  • Have passed Class 10/12 exam (or its equivalent) with at least 60% marks (or equivalent grade) in the academic year 2020-21
  • Have an annual family income of not more than INR 2,00,000 per annum from all sources
  • Be interested (and willing) to pursue higher education in the field of Medicine, Engineering, Graduation in any discipline, Integrated Courses, Diploma Course in any field or other equivalent courses, Vocational Courses through Government recognized Colleges/Institutes, or courses in Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs)
    Rewards and Benefits
  • An amount of INR 20,000 per annum will be awarded to the selected Regular Scholar and will be payable in three Quarterly installments
  • An amount of INR 10,000 per annum will be awarded for selected Special Girl Child pursuing studies in the 10+2 Course and scholarship shall be payable in three instalments.
